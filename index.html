About


Bayucaraka ITS Established

Bayucaraka ITS, founded in 2014 at the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) in Surabaya, focuses on developing Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The team has excelled in both national and international competitions, pushing the limits of UAV innovation to address real-world challenges.

Meaning of "Bayucaraka"

The name "Bayucaraka" combines the Sanskrit words "Vāyu" (air) and "Caraka" (wanderer), symbolizing the team's mission to explore new horizons in aerial technology and innovation.

Division

Bayucaraka ITS consists of divisions that collaborate to develop cutting-edge UAV technology and achieve success in competitions.

Technical

Bayucaraka ITS has a Technical division with two key subdivisions: Electrical and Mechanical, working together to advance UAV technology and excel in competitions.

Programming

Bayucaraka ITS has three key divisions: Technical (Electrical and Mechanical) and Programming, which focuses on C++, JavaScript, and Python for UAV development.

Official

Bayucaraka ITS is organized into Technical (Electrical and Mechanical), Programming, and Official divisions, with the Official division handling administration, sponsorship, media, and web development.

Achievement

Bayucaraka ITS has achieved numerous awards in national and international competitions, reflecting our expertise and commitment to UAV innovation.

SAFMC 2024


SAFMC 2024 (Singapore Amazing Flying Machine Competition) is an annual event where participants design and fly their own aerial vehicles. The competition features various categories, including fixed-wing planes, multi-rotor drones, and autonomous systems, encouraging creativity and technical skill in UAV design and operation. It aims to inspire innovation in aerospace technology among students and enthusiasts.

SAFMC 2024


TÜBİTAK (The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey) is an organization that promotes research, science, and technology in Turkey. One of its key events is the TÜBİTAK Science Olympiads, which are national competitions held in various scientific disciplines like mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science.

FIRA


FIRA (Federation of International Robot-soccer Association) is an international competition that focuses on robotics, particularly robot soccer. The event is held annually and brings together teams from around the world to compete in various categories related to robotics and artificial intelligence. The main goal of FIRA is to advance robotics research and development by providing a platform for testing, innovation, and collaboration.

KRTI 2024


KRTI (Kontes Robot Terbang Indonesia) is an annual competition organized by the Indonesian Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (Kemendikbudristek) that focuses on the development of aerial robotics. It aims to encourage innovation and enhance the skills of students in the field of unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) technology
